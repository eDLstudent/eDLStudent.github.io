Seaweed Farmer

Beautiful underwater forests of seaweed are entrancing to watch visually but did you know that seaweed farming is big business? Growing and harvesting seaweed can happen naturally or by fully controlling the life cycle of the algae. ... Seaweed plants can be a source of food as well as a raw material for agar and carrageenan products. There are many millions of tons of seaweed farmed every year. One of the coolest things about seaweed is that it is a carbon negative crop, meaning that it can help with the fight against climate change. My project explores data about the benefits of seaweed farming.
